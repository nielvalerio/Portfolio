FIG. 01 — SELF

Hello! My name is John Daniel V. Robles, a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate from City College of Calamba with a strong passion for software development, problem-solving, and building practical digital solutions. I enjoy turning ideas into functional applications and continuously expanding my knowledge by exploring new technologies and development practices.

Throughout my academic journey, I developed several full-stack web applications, mobile applications, and an AI-powered chatbot. My capstone project, QARMS, is a web-based records management system developed for our college's Quality Assurance and Records Management Office, featuring secure authentication, document management, CRUD operations, reporting and analytics, and multi-user collaboration. I also developed an AI chatbot designed to provide automated assistance and improve user interaction, allowing me to gain experience in integrating intelligent features into web applications. In addition, I built a campus navigation mobile application for Android, where I strengthened my mobile development and problem-solving skills by overcoming technical challenges throughout the development process.

I also completed my 600-hour on-the-job training at the Office of the College Registrar as an IT Support and Programmer. During my internship, I developed OCRMS, a web-based records management system with queue management, process automation, and role-based access control to improve office operations. The project was recognized with the Best OJT and Innovation Merit Award for its innovation and practical impact.

My technical skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Java, responsive web development, UI/UX design, database management, and Git/GitHub. I am currently seeking an opportunity to begin my professional career as a software developer, where I can contribute my technical skills, continue learning from experienced professionals, and develop innovative, reliable, and user-centered applications.
